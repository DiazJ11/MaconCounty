Public Safety & Emergency Information

In the event of an emergency, always dial 911 immediately.

For non-emergency assistance, residents can contact the appropriate public safety office during normal business hours.

  • Non-Emergency Dispatch: (555) 555-1212
  • Sheriff’s Office: (555) 555-3434
  • Fire Services (Non-Emergency): (555) 555-7878

This information is sample content for the redesigned Macon County website and is not associated with real phone numbers.

When to Call 911
  • Life-threatening medical emergencies
  • Fires or hazardous conditions
  • Crimes in progress or immediate danger
  • Serious traffic accidents
